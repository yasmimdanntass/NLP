Subject: video conference scheduling
In order to improve the video conferencing services offered to the enron
Community, the video conference group will be implementing the following
Procedural changes. These changes will be effective immediately.
Conference scheduling:
Please route all video conference requests via email to' videoconference'
(one word) or by phone to 713 - 345 - 8500 (x 5 - 8500). Once room reservations are
Complete, conferences will be scheduled and confirmation notices sent to the
Requestor via email. Each requestor will receive a confirmation notice the
Business day preceding scheduled conferences.
The following information will be required for all conference requests:
Requestor name and contact number
Sites to be included in the conference
Site contact names along with associated phone numbers
Date, time and duration of conference
Company number/rc or cost center of requestor
Preferred conference rooms (near - end and far - end)
Technical problems:
Should problems arise during a video conference, call 713 - 345 - 8555 and a
Technician will be immediately dispatched
Thanks in advance for your cooperation,
