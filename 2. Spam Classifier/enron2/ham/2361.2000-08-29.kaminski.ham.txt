Subject: apex conference 2000 update
Apex 2000 is an opportunity to join colleagues from around the globe for a
Conference on relevant, timely and strategic issues facing our industry.
Delegates and speakers from asia, australia, europe and north and south
Americas will be meeting in canada in october to discuss issues that will
Shape electrical deregulation in the foreseeable future. The conference is
Scheduled for october 25 and 26, 2000 in kananaskis, near calgary, alberta.
